Hamburger recipe

Ingredients

  • Hamburger buns
  • Ground meat
  • Sliced cheese
  • Onions
  • Tomato
  • Lettuce
  • Bacon
  • Preferred sauce

Preparation

  1. Use ground beef - grind your own or buy it ground, but 20% fat is ideal and keep it refrigerated until you're ready to use it.
  2. Shape the patties - 1” wider than the bun since they shrink on the grill.
  3. Get a good Sear - Once on the grill, let patties brown and sear well (3-5 min) before flipping, and do not press down on the burger save that for making Smash Burgers.
  4. Sauce - Apply a generous amount of sauce to both buns
  5. Toppings - Place pickles, lettuce, tomatoes, and red onion onto the bottom bun
  6. Patty - Place cheesy burger patty over the veggies and cover with top bun.

Tip: If you prefer a thicker, beefier patty, try making 1/3 lb patties, but keep in mind, the grilling time will be longer with thicker patties.

And there you go! You made a delicious burger! Go crazy!
