Ramen noodles recipe

Ingredients

  • Noodles
  • Broth
  • Vegetables
  • Seasoning
  • Chili oil
  • Sesame seeds
  • Scallions
  • Mushrooms
  • Chicken or pork

Preparation

  1. Boil your noodles - Put your noodles in a pot of boiling water. Stir them for 3-5 minutes.
  2. Prepare the vegetables - Put the veggies into the pot, together with the noodles
  3. Add the broth - Put the broth in the pot for umami taste
  4. Add the seasoning - Add your preferred seasoning into the pot.
  5. Take the noodles off the pot - Once the noodles and veggies are cooked, take them off the stove and put them in a bowl.
  6. Add the rest of ingredients - Decorate and enhance your bowl of noodles with sesame seeds and scallions for example.

Tip: Add some sriracha or chilli oil for that extra punch of spice.

And there you have it! A tasty bowl of noodles. Enjoy your dish!
